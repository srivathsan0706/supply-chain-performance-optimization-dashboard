# Supply Chain Performance Optimization Dashboard

## Overview
This project is a Supply Chain Performance Optimization Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze operational efficiency, supplier quality, logistics cost performance, and regional inventory risks across multiple cities and product categories.

The dashboard provides actionable insights for supply chain, procurement, logistics, and operations teams.

---

## Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to monitor supplier quality, transportation performance, inventory shortages, and operational KPIs through a centralized reporting system.

This dashboard was designed to:
- Improve operational visibility
- Identify procurement risks
- Analyze logistics inefficiencies
- Track inventory gaps
- Support business decision-making

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of:
- Revenue performance
- Defect rate
- Inspection pass rate
- Shipping efficiency
- Product category contribution

---

### 2. Regional Performance Analysis
Analyzes:
- City-level operational performance
- Shipping delays by location
- Revenue contribution by city
- Inventory stock gap risks  

---

### 3. City-Level Operational Drillthrough
Interactive drillthrough analysis page enabling deeper city-level operational investigation including:
- SKU defect tracking
- Supplier-specific quality analysis
- Inspection status monitoring
- Product category revenue contribution

---

### 4. City-Level Operational Analysis
Focuses on:
- SKU-level performance tracking
- Inspection status monitoring
- Product-level defect analysis
- Supplier-specific operational insights

---

### 5. Supplier Risk & Procurement Optimization
Evaluates:
- Supplier quality performance
- Manufacturing cost efficiency
- Procurement risk exposure
- Supplier defect contribution

---

### 5. Transportation Cost Optimization & Carrier Efficiency
Analyzes:
- Transportation mode efficiency
- Logistics cost trends
- Carrier route optimization
- Cost vs defect rate relationship

---

## Key Business Insights
- Skincare category generated the highest overall revenue contribution.
- Road transportation showed the highest defect contribution despite high logistics spending.
- Interactive drillthrough functionality enables operational teams to perform city-level root cause analysis directly from high-level dashboards.
- Supplier 3 demonstrated the best balance between manufacturing cost and quality performance.
- Delhi showed the highest inventory stock gap indicating replenishment risk.
- Chennai contributed significantly to shipping delays and operational inefficiencies.
